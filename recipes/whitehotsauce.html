White Hot Sauce

An Aberrant Hot Sauce

Incorporating this unconventional hot sauce into your dishes can elevate your culinary experience, inviting a sense of adventure and creativity into your cooking endeavors.

This recipe is written for chefs. The instructions are vague and intentionally left to interpretation.


White Hot Sauce

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • White Onion
  • Garlic
  • Cauliflower
  • White Tequila
  • Lime Juice
  • Serrano Ribs and Seeds
  • Cilantro Stems
  • Salt
  • Bay Leaf
  • Oil

Method:

  1. Simmer all ingredients.
  2. Remove bay leaf and cilantro stems.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Chill.
  5. Adjust consistency and season to taste.
