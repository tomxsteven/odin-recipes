RED MASHED POTATOES

An Aberrant Mash

Incorporating these unconventional mash potatoes into your dishes can elevate your culinary experience, inviting a sense of adventure and creativity into your cooking endeavors.

This recipe is written for chefs. The instructions are vague and intentionally left to interpretation.


Garlic Confit

Ingredients:

  • Garlic
  • Oil

Method:

  1. Submerge garlic in oil.
  2. Heat oil to ~180°F.
  3. Low simmer garlic until soft.

Piquillo Purée

Ingredients:

  • Piquillo Peppers
  • Onion
  • Garlic Confit
  • Oil

Method:

  1. Sauté all ingredients.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Adjust consistency and season to taste.

Red Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Cream
  • Butter
  • Piquillo Purée

Method:

  1. Cook potatoes until soft.
  2. Warm Cream.
  3. Cube and chill butter.
  4. Rice cooked potatoes with chilled butter.
  5. Fold in piquillo purée and warm cream.
  6. Adjust consistency and season to taste.
